Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish made with butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese . The sauce is rich and creamy, coating the long, flat noodles perfectly. It's a simple yet indulgent dish that's perfect for a special occasion or a cozy night in.

Alfredo sauce is supposed to taste mostly buttery, with the addition of the umami taste that comes from Parmesan . You could always add spicy stuff to it, but that will change the taste into something completely different.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: Of course, you’ll need fettuccine pasta.
  • Butter: This Alfredo sauce starts with two sticks of butter.
  • Cream: The rich sauce calls for almost a cup of heavy cream.
  • Seasonings: The fettuccine Alfredo is simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic salt.
  • Cheese: You’ll need Romano and Parmesan cheeses.

How to Make

Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade fettuccine Alfredo:

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
